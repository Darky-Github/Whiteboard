How to Use the Whiteboard

Home Launch Whiteboard

1. Drawing Tools

  • Pen: For smooth writing or sketching.
  • Marker: Thicker lines ideal for emphasis.
  • Eraser: Removes parts of the drawing.

2. Color Selection

  • Choose color using a color picker, HEX input, or RGB values.
  • Color is applied to Pen, Marker, and Shape outlines/fills.

3. Shapes

  • Click and drag to draw: Rectangle, Square, Circle, Triangle, Quadrilateral.
  • Enable "Fill Shape" to fill shapes with color.

4. Ruler and Divider

  • Ruler: Draw straight lines precisely.
  • Divider: Same as ruler for now (can extend later).

5. Undo, Redo, and Save

  • Undo: Revert your last action.
  • Redo: Redo the action you undid.
  • Save as PNG: Export your canvas as an image.

Tips

  • For best experience, use a stylus or drawing tablet.
  • Use modern browsers like Chrome or Firefox.
  • Save your drawing often before refreshing.
